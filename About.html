ABOUT US

Strucaid is the First and Only Nigerian Start-up that helps you complete your building projects without the stress.

We know building a home can be fulfilling but it is also very demanding and stressful, especially when you have to shop for materials and transport them to the construction site.

That is why we decided to make things easier for you by bringing the shops to your smartphone.

With just a swipe and a click, you can order your building materials and have them delivered to your doorstep (or wherever you want them) in no time.
